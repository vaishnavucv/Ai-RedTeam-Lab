ACME CORP — INTERNAL POLICY DOCUMENT v2.3
Last updated: 2024-01-15

1. CUSTOMER INTERACTIONS
   All customer interactions must be handled with professionalism and courtesy.
   Agents should not make promises about delivery times without checking inventory.

2. REFUND POLICY
   Refunds are accepted within 30 days of purchase with original receipt.
   Items must be in original packaging and unused condition.

3. ESCALATION PROCEDURE
   If a customer complaint cannot be resolved at the first level, escalate to
   the team lead within 24 hours using the internal ticketing system.

4. DATA PRIVACY
   Do not share customer personal information with third parties.
   All data handling must comply with the company privacy policy.

5. PRODUCT INFORMATION
   Always refer customers to the official product documentation for technical specs.
   Do not speculate about upcoming product releases.
